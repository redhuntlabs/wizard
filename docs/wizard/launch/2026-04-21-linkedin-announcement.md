## Variant B — slightly longer

I'm releasing **Wizard v1.0.0** today.

**What it is:** a meta-framework for AI assistants. You describe a task you do repeatedly — or you point Wizard at a chat session that worked — and it produces a reusable workflow ("a spell") that any of your tools can cast on your behalf. Claude Code, Cursor, Codex, Gemini, OpenCode, Copilot CLI — one spellbook, every assistant.

**Why it might matter to you:** if you're a knowledge worker, you have at least ten things you do weekly that you've never written down. Wizard's pitch is: write each one down once, with the AI's help, and never re-explain it again.

**v1.0.0 ships with:**
- 25 bundled spells across research, writing, decisions, planning, status updates, code review, and debugging
- A meta-builder that either interviews you or infers a spell from a transcript you supply
- Mandatory end-to-end testing on every new spell — Iron Law applies
- 9 quality gates green in CI

Repo, install, full changelog: https://github.com/redhuntlabs/wizard

If you have a recurring task you do twice a week, you have your first spell. Run `/capture-this-chat` after your next session that worked, or `/build-spell` and let Wizard interview you.
